A teleprompter that tracks your voice.

Paste a script. Hit Start. The prompter advances as you speak. Your script stays on this device. Voice tracking uses your browser's Speech Recognition (Chrome may process audio online).

Open Teleprompter How It Works

Voice Tracking

Uses your browser's Speech Recognition to keep your place word-by-word.

Auto-Scroll Mode

Prefer a fixed pace? Switch to auto-scroll and set your speed.

Phone Remote

Control Start/Stop, nudges, and speed from a second device on your Wi-Fi.

Mirror + Pop-Out

Flip the display for mirror rigs and pop the prompter into its own window.

Speech Recognition support is best in Chrome / Edge. Safari and Firefox may not support voice tracking.
